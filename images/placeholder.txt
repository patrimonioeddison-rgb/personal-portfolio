This directory is for your local images.

TO ADD YOUR OWN IMAGES:
1. Put your image files here (hero-bg.jpg, profile.jpg, project-1.jpg, etc.)
2. Update data/config.js to point to the new filenames:
   - images.hero: "images/hero-bg.jpg"
   - images.profile: "images/profile.jpg"
   - projects[].img: "images/project-1.jpg", etc.

Supported formats: .jpg, .png, .webp, .avif

For best results:
- Hero image: 1200x1400px
- Profile image: 800x800px  
- Project images: 800x600px

You can also use absolute URLs if you prefer (like unsplash.com or your own CDN).
